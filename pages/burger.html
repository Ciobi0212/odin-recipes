Description

Enjoy the perfect American classic with our Burger recipe. This mouthwatering delight features a juicy, seasoned beef patty nestled inside a soft, toasted bun. Top it with fresh lettuce, tomato slices, onions, and your favorite condiments for a satisfying and delicious homemade burger experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450 grams) ground beef (preferably 80% lean)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Cheese slices (usually American, Cheddar, or your choice)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to season the beef patties
  • Butter or oil for toasting buns (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by gathering all the necessary ingredients and utensils.
  2. Preheat your grill or stove top skillet to medium-high heat.
  3. While the grill or skillet is heating up, divide the ground beef into equal portions to form your burger patties. Season each patty with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. If you prefer, you can shape the patties into a round shape or use a burger press for consistency.
  5. Place the burger patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time to your desired level of doneness.
  6. While the patties are cooking, you can optionally toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster until they are lightly browned.
  7. When the burgers are almost done, add a slice of cheese to each patty and allow it to melt slightly by covering the grill or skillet with a lid.
  8. Remove the burger patties from the heat and let them rest for a minute while you prepare the toppings.
  9. Assemble your burgers by placing a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each bun, followed by a burger patty with melted cheese, tomato slices, and onion rings.
  10. Add any additional condiments of your choice, such as ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.
  11. Finish by placing the top half of the burger bun on the assembled ingredients.
  12. Insert a toothpick or skewer through the center to hold the burger together, if desired.
  13. Your delicious homemade burger is now ready to serve!
